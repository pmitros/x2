Useful Formulas and Definitions

Review the following and mark the item as complete when you are done:

  1. Variance of a sum of independent random variables:

    Var(X+Y) = Var(X) + Var(Y)

  2. Variance of an additive constant

    Var(X+c) = Var(X)

  3. Variance of a multiplicative constant

    Var(cX) = c²Var(X)

  4. The sum of two independent Normal random variables is another random variable:


    X₁~N(μ₁,σ₁²)
    X₂~N(μ₂,σ₂²)
    X₁+X₂~N(μ₁+μ₂, σ₁²+ σ₂²)
